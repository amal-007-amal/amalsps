The Community of Developers (CODe) is the students association of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Christ College of Engineering. It was established in the year 2016. The technical events comprise of several workshops such as Android development, Web development and preparation towards competitive coding. CODE also conducts South Indias only Beach Hackathon - Beach Hack. The Alumni continue to contribute to this forum by guiding their juniors on academics, placement and career. This association provides a space for students to develop their leadership & management skills, exhibit & share their technical skills. Overall, CODE tightness the bond between CSE students, alumni & staff.
